From 3.5 to 4.9 Stars in 90 Days

How Premier Painting Co. transformed their reputation and now commands $20K+ exterior projects

The Challenge

Premier Painting Co. had been in business for 12 years with excellent craftsmanship, but one bad review from a difficult customer was killing their online reputation. At 3.5 stars, they were losing bids to newer competitors with better ratings.

The Solution

Estimate Wave implemented a comprehensive reputation management strategy, focusing on community engagement and showcasing Premier's exceptional work across local homeowner groups.

The Results

  • Rating improved from 3.5 to 4.9 stars
  • Average project value increased to $20K+
  • Booked solid for 3 months ahead
  • 73% close rate on estimates

The Full Story

Background

John Martinez, owner of Premier Painting Co., had built his business on referrals and quality work. But in today's digital age, his online reputation wasn't reflecting the true quality of his work. One unfair review from a customer who wanted free work was costing him thousands in lost projects.

"I knew we did amazing work. Our past clients loved us. But new customers would see that 3.5-star rating and go with someone else. It was frustrating watching inferior painters win jobs just because they had better online ratings." - John Martinez, Owner

The Estimate Wave Approach

We started by auditing Premier's entire online presence and discovered they had dozens of happy customers who had never left reviews. Our strategy focused on three key areas:

  1. Reputation Amplification: We systematically reached out to past satisfied customers and made it easy for them to share their positive experiences.
  2. Community Positioning: We positioned John as the go-to painting expert in local homeowner groups, sharing tips and showcasing beautiful transformations.
  3. Trust Building Content: We created before/after showcases that demonstrated Premier's attention to detail and superior finishing work.

The Transformation

Within 30 days, Premier's rating jumped to 4.3 stars. By day 90, they had achieved a 4.9-star rating with over 40 new positive reviews. But the real transformation was in the quality of leads.

"The leads completely changed. Before, people would call asking for the cheapest price. Now they call saying 'We've heard you're the best, when can you start?' We're booking $20K+ exterior projects regularly." - John Martinez, Owner
