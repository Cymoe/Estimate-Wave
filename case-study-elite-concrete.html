Booked Solid with $15K+ Driveways

How Elite Concrete & Turf eliminated tire kickers and now books premium projects 3 months in advance

The Challenge

Elite Concrete & Turf was wasting hours each week on estimates for price shoppers who wanted the cheapest option. Despite excellent work, they were competing on price instead of quality.

The Solution

Estimate Wave positioned Elite as the premium choice for decorative concrete and artificial turf, building trust through strategic community presence and showcasing their superior craftsmanship.

The Results

  • Zero tire kickers - 100% qualified leads
  • Average project value: $15K+ driveways
  • Booked solid 3 months ahead
  • 80% close rate on estimates

The Full Story

Background

Mike and Sarah Chen had built Elite Concrete & Turf on a foundation of superior craftsmanship and innovative designs. They specialized in decorative concrete driveways and premium artificial turf installations. But they were stuck in a cycle of competing with budget contractors on price.

"We'd spend 2-3 hours on detailed estimates, creating custom designs, only to have homeowners go with someone charging half our price for basic gray concrete. It was exhausting and demoralizing." - Sarah Chen, Co-Owner

The Problem with Traditional Lead Generation

Elite was using HomeAdvisor and Google Ads, which sent them leads that went to 4-5 other contractors. Homeowners were shopping purely on price, not understanding the difference between basic concrete and Elite's decorative installations.

The Estimate Wave Strategy

We completely shifted Elite's positioning from competing on price to being THE premium choice. Our approach included:

  1. Premium Positioning: We showcased Elite's work in affluent neighborhood groups where homeowners valued quality and curb appeal.
  2. Education-First Content: We created content explaining the difference between basic and decorative concrete, positioning Elite as the experts.
  3. Social Proof at Scale: We amplified stories from thrilled customers whose property values increased after Elite's installations.
  4. Trust Before Contact: By the time homeowners reached out, they already understood Elite's value proposition and expected premium pricing.

The Immediate Impact

Within two weeks, the quality of leads completely transformed. Instead of "How much for a basic driveway?", inquiries became "We love the stamped concrete driveway you did on Oak Street. When can you start ours?"

"The first qualified lead we got through Estimate Wave was for a $22,000 driveway with decorative borders and custom coloring. They didn't even ask for other quotes. They said, 'You're the company everyone recommends.'" - Mike Chen, Co-Owner

Building Momentum

As Elite completed more premium projects in targeted neighborhoods, the referral effect multiplied. Neighbors saw the stunning transformations and wanted the same quality for their homes.
